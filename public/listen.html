Radio Free DeLand is a living, breathing broadcast — open lines, real voices, and the unscripted pulse of a Florida community tuning in at every hour of the day and night.

How the Format Works

Every hour on Radio Free DeLand sounds a little different — and that's exactly the point. The show runs continuously, welcoming callers from morning commutes, afternoon breaks, and the quiet stretch of the overnight. There's no script, no fixed roster of guests, only an open line and whoever picks up the phone.

Local news of the moment finds its way into the conversation naturally. A neighbor calls in about a road closure on New York Avenue. Someone else reflects on a story that's been on their mind all week. By 2 a.m. the tone shifts — slower, more reflective — the kind of radio that feels like a late-night drive through familiar streets.

This is community radio / broadcasting at its most direct: no gatekeeping, no editorial filter between the city and the microphone. DeLand speaks, and the signal carries it.

  • Overnight & reflective hours — When the city quiets down

On The Air — 24 Hours A Day

Broadcast Rhythm

Radio Free DeLand never goes dark. Every hour of the day carries a different mood, a different voice — but the line is always open. Tune in, call in, become part of the signal.

12 AM – 6 AM Overnight

Overnight Voices

When DeLand goes quiet, the airwaves come alive. The overnight hours belong to the night owls, the insomniacs, and anyone who finds the world easier to talk about in the dark. Soft, unhurried conversation drifts across the dial — local stories, distant thoughts, and the kind of honesty that only comes after midnight. The phone lines are always open.

6 AM – Noon Morning

Sunrise Community Check-In

Florida mornings have a particular quality — warm light, humid air, and the feeling that the day could go anywhere. Our sunrise block is a community gathering point: local news, neighborhood voices, and the kind of conversation that makes a small city feel like a living room. Call in with what's on your mind before the day takes over.

Noon – 8 PM Daytime

Daytime Local Pulse

The heartbeat of Volusia County plays out in real time. Afternoons on Radio Free DeLand track the rhythm of everyday life — from downtown happenings to statewide conversations that ripple back to our corner of Florida. Lively, direct, and always tuned to what's actually happening, this is radio / broadcasting as it was meant to be: present, local, and alive.

8 PM – Midnight Evening

After-Dark Free-Form Call-Ins

No agenda, no script. The evening hours on Radio Free DeLand are pure open format — a free-form space where callers lead and the conversation follows wherever it needs to go. Music, debate, personal stories, or just the sound of someone who needed to be heard. The dial is yours. Pick up the phone.

The line is open around the clock — call in at any hour and become part of the broadcast.
