Like Your Neighborhood

Radio Free DeLand is a round-the-clock call-in radio show rooted in the rhythms, voices, and stories of Central Florida. We believe that broadcasting — real radio / broadcasting in its most human form — should feel spontaneous, immediate, and deeply local.

Whether it's two in the afternoon or two in the morning, someone is on the air and the lines are open. Our format is simple: tune in, pick up the phone, and become part of the conversation. No gatekeepers, no scripts — just DeLand talking to itself, one call at a time.

Shaped by the culture, characters, and unhurried pace of Florida living, Radio Free DeLand is a platform built for the community, by the community. We carry local voices the way the humid air carries sound at dusk — warm, close, and impossible to ignore.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Radio Free DeLand isn't just a broadcast — it's a handshake with every person in Volusia County who ever wanted to be heard. These aren't corporate talking points. They're the reason we keep the mic open at 3 a.m.

01

Open to Everyone, Always

The airwaves belong to the people. Whether you're calling in from a porch in Stetson Hills or a late-night diner on US-17, there's a seat at the table and a line kept open for you — twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week, no cover charge.

02

Honest, Unfiltered Conversation

We don't script our guests or polish away the rough edges. Real talk from real neighbors is what makes radio worth listening to. We hold space for disagreement, nuance, and the kind of honesty you only get when the mic is live and nobody's performing.

03

Rooted Right Here in DeLand

We're proud to broadcast from the Athens of Florida. Our stories come from these streets, these festivals, these moss-draped neighborhoods. Local voices aren't a feature — they're the whole point. We cover what the big stations skip, because this community deserves its own signal.
