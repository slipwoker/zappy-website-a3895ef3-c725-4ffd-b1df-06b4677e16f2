    A Station Born From the Neighborhood

    Radio Free DeLand isn't broadcasting from some distant studio tower — we're woven into the fabric of this city. DeLand is our signal source: its live oaks and brick-paved downtown, its Stetson students and longtime locals, its Friday-night energy and 3 a.m. quiet. Every hour of our 24/7 call-in show reflects the community that keeps us on the air.

    • Located in the heart of DeLand, Florida — Volusia County's cultural crossroads, where small-city warmth meets a surprisingly vibrant arts and music scene.
    • Broadcasting live around the clock — our signal reaches listeners across Volusia County, Central Florida, and anywhere a browser or radio can tune in.
    • Open to every voice — our call-in lines are active 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, because community conversation doesn't keep office hours.
    • Proudly independent — Radio Free DeLand is a community-driven broadcasting effort, shaped by local stories, local hosts, and local listeners.

    ON AIR ANSWERS

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about tuning in, calling in, and joining the conversation on Radio Free DeLand.

    Q

    How do I get in touch with the show?

    The easiest way to reach us is by phone — our call-in line is open around the clock, every day of the year. You can also send us a message through the contact form on this page, or reach out via our social channels. We read everything and love hearing from the community.

    Q

    When is the show live?

    Radio Free DeLand broadcasts live 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — no gaps, no reruns. Whether it's 2 in the afternoon or 3 in the morning, there's always a live host ready to take your call. That's the whole idea: community radio that never sleeps.

    Q

    What kinds of calls fit the program?

    We're wide open. Talk about what's on your mind — local DeLand news, Florida culture, personal stories, opinions, questions, or just a friendly chat. Our format is built around real voices from real people, so there's no wrong reason to call in. Keep it respectful and we'll keep the line open.

    Q

    Can community members share local updates?

    Absolutely — that's what we're here for. If you have a local event, a neighborhood update, a story worth telling, or news from your corner of Volusia County, call in and share it. Radio Free DeLand runs on community voices. Your update could be the one someone else needed to hear.

    Q

    Do I need to be a radio professional to participate?

    Not at all. Radio Free DeLand was built for everyday people, not polished broadcasters. First-time callers are always welcome. If you're nervous, don't be — our hosts will guide the conversation. All you need is something to say and a phone to say it from.

    Q

    Where can I tune in from?

    You can listen live through our website stream from anywhere in the world. Local listeners in the DeLand area can also catch us on the dial. Whether you're driving through Florida or streaming from across the country, Radio Free DeLand is always on and always accessible.
